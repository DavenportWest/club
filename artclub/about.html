Digital Arts Club

The Digital Arts Club is an after school club that normally runs every Tuesday, from 3:00 to 4:00 P.M. Every Tuesday around 3:00 and 4:00 P.M. students are able to make art using computer programs such as PhotoShop and Adobe Students are also able to enter there art works into fun contest, and students were able to go to the Figge Art Museum for a field trip. Everyting that students do in Digital Arts Club is fun and educational. Students are able to learn how to make interesting peices of art uding computer programing, which can help them to get a unique job and some places even offer scholarships.
